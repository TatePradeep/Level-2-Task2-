Biography

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor born on January 21, 1986, in Patna, Bihar. He started his career as a television actor, appearing in popular shows such as "Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil" and "Pavitra Rishta". He gained widespread recognition for his portrayal of Manav Deshmukh in the latter show, which ran for several years. In 2013, Rajput made his debut in Bollywood with the film "Kai Po Che!", which was a critical and commercial success. He went on to star in several other films, including "Shuddh Desi Romance", "PK", "Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!", "MS Dhoni: The Untold Story" (for which he received critical acclaim and several awards for his portrayal of the titular character), "Raabta" and "Chhichhore".
